What is DataFest?

ASA DataFest™ is a weekend-long data analysis competition where teams of students work to extract insight from a rich, complex, and surprise dataset. It’s a great opportunity to showcase your skills, network, and have fun!

Schedule

  • Friday: Kickoff & dataset reveal
  • Saturday: Full day of analysis with mentor check-ins
  • Sunday: Presentations and awards

Workshops

FAQ

Who can participate? Undergraduate and graduate students from any major.

Do I need a team? You can sign up solo — we’ll help match you with others.
